# Welcome to Tesseract

Your thoughts deserve better than a notes app. Tesseract is a local-first, AI-enhanced markdown notebook — a thinking environment where you write, interrogate your own ideas, and when needed, unleash a council of AI personas to tear them apart.

No cloud. No lock-in. Just you, your markdown, and some uncommonly sharp AI.

---

## Modes

Switch between them from the Activity Bar on the left.

**Notes**
Write and organize markdown notes in a rich editor. Everything auto-saves to `~/Tesseract/Notes/` as plain `.md` files. Yours forever, no subscription required.

**AI**
A sidebar companion that reads your current note and actually talks to you about it. Hit `/write` and it'll rewrite the whole note for you. Useful when your draft is alive but ugly.

**Council**
Three AI personas — `Visionary`, `Skeptic`, `Pragmatist` — convene and debate your idea from different angles. If your plan survives the Skeptic, it might actually be good.

---

## Command Palette

`Ctrl+K` — your one shortcut to rule them all. From here you can:

- Bump the editor font size up or down
- Toggle autocomplete on/off
- Flip between light and dark theme
- Pick a color theme

---

## Keyboard Shortcuts

| Shortcut             | Action                         |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| `Ctrl+K`             | Command palette                |
| `Ctrl+B`             | Toggle notes sidebar           |
| `Ctrl+Shift+B`       | Toggle AI sidebar              |
| `Alt+A`              | Toggle code autocomplete       |
| `Ctrl+Space` / `Tab` | Accept autocomplete suggestion |

---

## Color Themes

`Ctrl+K` → search `Pick Theme`. A growing set of themes, each one overriding the full app palette via CSS custom properties. Find one that doesn't make you want to close the laptop.

---

## Your Notes Are Just Files

Plain `.md` files sitting in `~/Tesseract/Notes/`. Create, edit, delete, organize — no proprietary format, no account, no extraction ritual if you ever leave. They were always yours.

## Tips

- Nested lists need a 2-space indent
- Blank line between blocks keeps the renderer happy
- Files live at `~/Tesseract/Notes/` — always plain `.md`
